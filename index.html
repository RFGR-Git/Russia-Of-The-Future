About Us

Our Journey: An Interactive Timeline

Explore the history of Russia of the Future through our dynamic, horizontally scrollable timeline. Each milestone is represented by a node that expands on click to reveal details.

1999 – The Spark

Informal circles of reform-minded thinkers begin to gather in Moscow, dreaming of a Russia beyond corruption, chaos, and compromise.

2000 – Turning Point

The presidential transition awakens a deeper need for modernization and independent political vision.

2002 – Grassroots Awakening

The movement spreads—students, workers, and veterans organizing in Saint Petersburg, Kazan, and Novosibirsk.

2003 – Organizational Foundation

Party formally registers. The Charter is published and ratified by over 40,000 supporters nationwide.

March 2004 – First National Push

First campaigns launched with limited resources but bold resolve. Public support grows daily.

Our Mission

To build a prosperous, innovative, and just Russia, empowering every citizen to reach their full potential and ensuring our nation stands strong and respected on the global stage. We are committed to fostering unity, progress, and a vibrant future for all.

Our Vision

A Russia where technology drives progress, culture flourishes, and every voice is heard. We envision a society built on principles of fairness, sustainability, and collective well-being, leading the way in global innovation and human development.
